listening

been absolutely thrashing to the hellaverse soundtrack. i've been remarkably boring this month, as i've been busy with wild happenstance. instead, we can talk about Die With a Smile[1] by Lady Gaga[2] and Bruno Mars[3], released as a standalone single on August 16, 2024. It became Spotify's most-streamed song of 2025[4] and is still topping official charts.

in my opinion, it's about damn time the dinosaurs went mecha-mode and wiped the charts clean of the slop[5] that plagues modern billboards. the only other noteable thing from 2025 that's continued charting into 2026 has been the k-pop demon hunter's stuff.
